# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or career stage.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and scientifically honest.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Critique ideas and results rather than people.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and unpublished research.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when they affect a discussion or review.

Harassment, discriminatory conduct, personal attacks, deliberate disruption,
and publication of another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `rmaples3@gatech.edu`. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may
edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or take other action
appropriate to the circumstances.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, review spaces,
and project-related public events or communications.
